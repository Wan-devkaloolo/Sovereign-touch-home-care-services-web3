Quick Tips
✦Clean top to bottom — dust settles downward.
✦Let cleaning products sit 2–3 minutes before wiping.
✦Microfibre cloths outperform paper towels on glass.
✦Clean grout monthly to prevent mould from setting.
✦Air out rooms for 10 mins after cleaning.
