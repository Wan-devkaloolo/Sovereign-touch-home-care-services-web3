Our Story

Built on trust,
delivered with pride.

Sovereign Touch was born from a simple belief: that every home and office in Kampala deserves to be treated with the same care and precision as the finest residences in the world.

How We Started

From one mop to
Kampala's most
trusted clean.

Sovereign Touch started in 2021 with one team, a handful of clients in Kololo, and a conviction that cleaning services in Kampala could be so much better — more reliable, more thorough, and worthy of the premium homes we were being invited into.

Word spread. Clients in Naguru, Bugolobi, and Muyenga started calling. Property managers began sending us their most valuable listings. Corporate offices asked for monthly retainers. We grew because we showed up on time, did the work completely, and never cut corners.

Today we serve over 200 households and businesses across Kampala. The promise hasn't changed: we treat your home the way you'd want it treated if you weren't watching.
